FOR THOSE GOING THROUGH A ROUGH TIME...

This site is created to bridge the gap between young Asian Americans and their parents who are first generation Asian immigrants. It helps guide them through their childhood by allowing them to understand their parents in terms of their own beliefs and upbringing and why they act the way they do. The site contains stories of older 2nd generation Asian Americans who have gone through the same upbringing to give insight as to how they dealt with some issues when growing up.
